## FAQ

**What is the Local SEO Performance Hub?**
An AI-first SaaS concept for local-business visibility: it tracks rankings, reviews, and citations and rolls them into one Visibility Score (0–100). Built for SMBs and the agencies managing many of them.

**Is it built?**
Not yet — it's a parked concept with detailed documentation. This repo is the thinking made public: system architecture, API design, AI architecture, pitch deck, coding standards, CI/CD.

**Why publish a parked concept?**
Transparency and portfolio. The design work is real and reusable even before the product is; nothing here is a secret.

**Who is it for?**
Small and medium local businesses, and the agencies that manage local SEO across multiple client locations.
